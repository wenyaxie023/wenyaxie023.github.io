I am currently a research assistant at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, under the guidance of Benyou Wang and Feng Jiang. I earned my BSc from Wuhan University in 2021.

## Research Interest

My primary focus is on natural language processing. I am currently dedicated to developing Large Language Models (LLMs) that are not only reliable but also robust.

## Other Interests

As an enthusiast of multidisciplinary studies, I also spend my leisure time diving into books on sociology, political economy, philosophy, and literature. Sometimes, these readings inspire me. By reading, I establish a deeper understanding of the world's patterns, and I hope to leverage this understanding to contribute to more holistic and innovative solutions in my field.
